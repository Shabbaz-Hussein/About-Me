My Childhood

Memorable Childhood Experiences

During class 6 in primary, I remember surprisingly reuniting with an old neighbour from 4 years ago. We were at a school fun day and I had caught her keenly staring at me with interest. I chatted her up and she jogged my memory about us being old neighbours. It was truly a fun moment as I always believed moving out was always akin to parting goodbye for good. That day how wrong I was proven.
